## 🌟 Overview

CRM Workspace is a modern enterprise-grade Customer Relationship Management and Operations Platform designed to help organizations streamline customer interactions, manage projects, improve team productivity, and automate internal business workflows.

The platform combines CRM, Project Management, Workforce Management, Ticketing, Attendance Tracking, Reporting, and Analytics into one unified ecosystem.

Whether you're a startup, agency, educational institution, service provider, or growing enterprise, CRM Workspace provides the tools needed to manage operations efficiently.
